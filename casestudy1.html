Project Overview

The Product

Camping E-commerce is an app that allow users to buy the camping tools as well as can view the camping tools and information about them. User can also buy the camping essentials.

Project information

  • Category: App design
  • Project Duration: Feb 1 - June 5, 2025

PROBLEM

These days people are using instagram, facebook and other platform to share their camping tools for selling to the world. This is convenient to the people but for the camping lover who like to visit and go on trek and want to explore more about the place have difficult time in searching for camping tools at those platform. Their time is wasted by searching for the camping essential on online platform.

THE GOAL

Design an app that allow user to buy their camping tool and get more information about the camping experience.

ROLE

UX/UI Designer. [UI (user interface), UX (user experience)]

Responsibilities

User research, User persona, Problem statement, User journey map, Competitive audit, Ideation activitie, User flow, Storyboard, Low fidelity wireframe, Low fidelity prototypes, High fidelity mockups, High fidelity prototypes, Usability studies

UNDERSTANDING THE USER

User research: Summary

Conducted interviews and created empathy maps help understand users needs, behaviours and motivations. My goal for conducting primary research is to identified user group. The most campers were adult, who want to spend time to get information about camping and buy tools for their camping.

User Research: Pain Point

01

Easy

User do not have to search for camping tools from different platform.

02

More info

User want to see more information about the camping tools and experience.

03

Share

User want to share their experience to different people.

Pema

Problem statement:

Pema is a first-time camper from Thimphu, I wanted to camp in Haa Valley last summer but struggled to find all the gear I needed in one place. I had to buy a tent from a shop in Norzin Lam and a sleeping bag from a different store in Olakha.

Ugyen

Problem statement:

Ugyen is a trekking enthusiast from Paro, I often trek to Bumdra and Jomolhari, but finding quality tents and jackets in Bhutan is difficult. I order from India, but shipping takes weeks. I wish there was a trusted online store in Bhutan.

DESIGN

Paper wireframe

I sketched paper wireframe of homepage, upload, login and signup page to explore various options for how to organize the information. I made notes on each screen of what I thought worked best, and incorporated those ideas into the digital low fidelity wireframes.



Digital wireframe

Next, I moved to Figma to create digital wireframes for all of the screens. Using a basic design system, I was able to create low fidelity wireframes that were still clean and organized. The homepage serves as a central hub where users can access a quick dashboard. The bottom navigation gives users options to buy the items, search for camping items, and access the platform. The bottom navigation includes tabs to each of the main pages of the app.

Going Forward
